# South Trail — scene script · "The Long Light" (Q2 + Q3)

The fleshed-out dialogue + staging for Map 2 ([design page](south-trail.md)).
Conventions as in the [Rim Hub script](rim-hub.scenes.md): voice-blips,
*(staging in italics)*, cozy-comedic tone (STORY §2), Vesper is a **tsundere**.
Markers (e.g. `SUNSHELF`, `THE OVERLOOK`) are the greybox labels.

**Cast on this map:** **Vesper** (you), **Gemma** (debuts here — the chuuni
succubus-cosplaying rival architect), **Lumi** (radio only), **Thread Imps**
(Gemma's "minions," who are not), and the leak-creatures (**Fold Slimes**,
**Pebble Drones**, **Cactus Satellites**). The two quests interleave: **Q2** (the
idol's gem, side) runs the length of the trail; **Q3** (Gemma) is the finale.

---

## S1 — "The Ask"  *(Q2 hook · marker: `TRAILHEAD (from Rim)`)*

**Staging.** Vesper steps off the portal onto the **`TRAILHEAD`** shelf; the
festival noise drops away to wind and the hum of a thicker leak. Her comms chime
— **Lumi**, breathless, over a crackly idol-grade radio.

> **LUMI:** *(radio)* Record giiirl! Okay don't freak out — you know my finale
> light? The big sparkly Gem on the stage? A blob *ate* it. A blue hoppy blob.
> It went down YOUR trail.
> **VESPER:** A Fold Slime took your… prop.
> **LUMI:** It's not a prop, it's the GEM, it runs the whole closer! And — *(beat)*
> — okay so it might've split. Into like. Three blobs. Each with a piece.
> **VESPER:** *(pinching the bridge of her nose)* You want me to herd three
> reality-leaks down a canyon to recover stage lighting.
> **LUMI:** *(brightly)* For the **record!** ☆
> **VESPER:** *(long pause)* …I was going down anyway. This isn't a favor.
> **LUMI:** Totally not a favor. Byeee! *(click)*
> **VESPER:** *(to herself)* …It's a little bit a favor.

*Purpose:* start Q2 with comedy + the Fold-Slime "splits" mechanic justified
diegetically; keep the Lumi rapport; tsundere "not a favor (it's a favor)."

---

## S2 — "Down the Long Light"  *(descent · markers: `SWITCHBACKS`)*

**Staging.** The **`SWITCHBACKS`** drop away in one-way ledges; the canyon opens
on the right, energy veins glittering in the far walls. First **Fold Slimes** hop
the path (the Q2 targets — slice clean or they multiply). Movement is the star
here; barks reward flow.

**First slime split** (you hit one and it divides):
> **VESPER:** *(a slime splits in two)* — of course it splits. *(deadpan)* Lumi.
> Your light is inside a self-replicating problem.

**Flow barks** (on a good flash-jump chain):
> **VESPER:** *(almost enjoying it)* …Acceptable trajectory. *(beat)* Very
> acceptable, actually.

*Purpose:* teach the descent + the split mechanic in motion; let Vesper start
*visibly* having fun while denying it (the core tsundere comedy).

---

## S3 — "Filing"  *(first horde · marker: `SUNSHELF (horde arena)`, `CACTUS HAZARD`)*

**Staging.** The **`SUNSHELF`** opens into the first real **horde** — Fold Slimes
and **Pebble Drones** in generous excess, **Cactus Satellites** dotting the floor
as orbiting-spike hazards to fight around. This is the power-fantasy intro: carve
a line, build momentum, pop the crowd into stardust. Mostly it's a *blast.* But
once — **once** — let the unease land, then leave it.

**The cozy/spectacle read** (mid-horde, as it's going well):
> **VESPER:** *(carving through them, genuinely lit up)* — and **down,** and
> down, and— ha. Okay. *This* the spec did not prepare me for.

**The single dread hint** (after the crowd's cleared, the shelf suddenly quiet —
play it straight, one beat, then back to cozy):
> **VESPER:** *(quiet, looking at the stardust settling)* …I keep saying I'm
> "filing them back." *(beat)* That's the word they'd use, isn't it. Upstairs.
> For a whole place. *(she shakes it off)* …Not my department. *(re-pins hat)*
> Three blobs. Where's the third.

*Purpose:* sell the spectacle pillar; plant the proto-Sweep dread **once**,
lightly (per STORY §2 calibration), via Vesper's own word choice — no monster, no
music sting, just a thought she puts down. Then immediately back to the Q2 chase.

## S4 — "The Trick Wall"  *(secret · markers: `TP-WALL (hidden)`, `SUN-CACHE`)*

**Staging.** The **`CRACKED SPAN`** is a flash-jump gauntlet; partway across, a
suspiciously plain **`TP-WALL`** dead-ends a ledge. Teleporting **mode B**
(phase) through it reveals the **`SUN-CACHE`** vault — fat stardust + a
cactus-bloom farmable. Optional; rewards the player who pokes at the world.

> **VESPER:** *(facing the dead-end wall)* …The strata's wrong here. It's
> *rendered,* not real. *(phases through)* Hm. *(finds the cache)* A pocket the
> seam forgot to finish. *(pleased despite herself)* …I'm keeping this. Survey
> equipment. *(it is not survey equipment.)*

*Purpose:* teach phase-teleport as exploration; reward curiosity; one more small
worldbuilding touch (the seam's "unfinished" pockets) that pays toward the dread
later without being heavy.

---

## S5 — "Domain of the Dread Duchess"  *(Q3 boss · markers: `THE OVERLOOK`, `GEMMA'S THRONE`)*

**Staging.** The trail opens onto **`THE OVERLOOK`**, a broad ledge over the gulch
drop. **GEMMA** is *waiting* — perched on a folding camp chair she has hard-light-
rendered into a gothic "throne," ringed by hard-light "dread banners," with three
**Thread Imps** flitting nearby that she is **certain** are her loyal minions.
She has clearly been rehearsing this entrance for a week. The whole fight is a
**comedy**; the difficulty is low; the joke is her commitment.

### Entrance (the monologue)

> **GEMMA:** *(rising, wings flaring, sparkles everywhere)* So. You've come at
> last. *(dramatic point)* **TREMBLE** — for I am **Gemma, Archduchess of the
> Sundered Veil, Sovereign of Sweet Ruin, She Who Folds the Dark!** And this
> canyon is now my **eternal domai—**
> **VESPER:** *(flat)* Last week you were "Duchess of the Abyssal Fold."
> **GEMMA:** *(thrown)* I'm — it's an **iterative title,** Vesper, it's a creative
> process, you wouldn't—
> **VESPER:** You can't claim domain over an **active survey site.** There's a
> moratorium. It's on page **four.**
> **GEMMA:** *(shrieking)* **NOBODY READS PAGE FOUR!** *(composing herself,
> gesturing at the Imps)* Behold my legion! My **creatures of darkness!**
> **VESPER:** *(watching an Imp drift obliviously past Gemma's head)* …They don't
> work for you.
> **GEMMA:** They **absolutely** work for me. *(to the Imps, sweetly)* Minions?
> *(the Imps ignore her completely)* …They're shy.

### The duel (banter over the fight)

*Phase 1 — telegraphed hard-light "abyssal" projectiles, slow, dodgeable; her
shield drops whenever she pauses for a dramatic line, which is constantly.*

> **GEMMA:** Taste the **VOID!** *(lobs a slow glowing orb)*
> **VESPER:** *(sidesteps)* That's a stabilizer pulse. Standard kit. You
> reskinned the **menu icon.**
> **GEMMA:** I **themed** it!

*Phase 2 — down a third, she drops the sing-song, tries for real (faster,
sloppier, genuinely a little dangerous, extremely whiny). Teleport-phase her big
"ultimate" beam and punish the recovery.*

> **GEMMA:** Okay — okay, no more games! Witness my **ULTIMATE—**
> **VESPER:** *(phases through the beam, lands a hit)*
> **GEMMA:** *(deflating mid-incantation)* — **ow.** Okay that's— you can't just
> *do that,* there's supposed to be a— a dramatic **exchange** first—
> **VESPER:** This **is** the exchange.

### Defeat (the deflation + the fondness)

> **GEMMA:** *(on the ground, wings sparking, hard-light throne flickering out)*
> …Fine. **FINE.** You win this round, Auditor. *(beat, smaller)* …It's a good
> bit though, right? The throne? I worked on the throne.
> **VESPER:** *(a long pause; the tsundere mask slips one millimeter)* …The
> throne was fine, Gemma.
> **GEMMA:** *(instantly radiant)* **I KNEW IT.** *(scrambling up, re-inflating)*
> This isn't over! I shall return — in a venue with **superior production
> values!** *(flops backward off the overlook into the gulch updraft)* FAREWELLLL—
> **VESPER:** *(peering over the edge after her, quietly)* …Land on something
> soft, you idiot. *(beat)* …For the record.

**Aftermath.** Gemma's exit leaves a half-melted "dread banner" (a cosmetic /
lore gag pickup) and the down-portal to **Whisper Gulch** humming open in her
wake (Q4, future). The third Fold Slime — the last Gem shard — was cowering
behind the throne the whole time. Easy grab.

> **VESPER:** *(collecting the last shard)* There you are. *(re-assembling Lumi's
> Gem)* …One pop idol's light show. Recovered. *(beat)* This is what I trained
> for. Truly.

*Purpose:* the big comedic set-piece; establish Gemma fully (chuuni, loser,
secretly beloved); the **mutual fondness** undertone (Vesper's "the throne was
fine" / "land on something soft"); resolve Q2's last shard; tee up Q4 without
weight.

## S6 — "Curtain"  *(Q2 payoff · radio, or back at the Rim stage)*

**Staging.** As Vesper heads back (or via radio), Lumi gets her Gem back in time
for the evening set. Short, warm, funny.

> **LUMI:** *(radio, hearing the Gem chime back online)* — IT'S BACK! The closer's
> back! Record girl, you absolute LEGEND, I could KISS you—
> **VESPER:** *(alarmed)* That won't be nec—
> **LUMI:** —metaphorically! For the **record!** *(beat, softer)* …Hey. Thanks.
> For real. The town needed the lights more than it needed the song.
> **VESPER:** *(gruff, pleased)* …It was on the way. *(beat)* Break a leg.

*Purpose:* warm button on Q2; deepen Lumi↔Vesper; reinforce the cozy north star
(the town, the lights) before the trail down into the dread of the Gulch (Q4).

## Ambient & idle

- **Gemma post-defeat barks** (if you revisit the overlook before Q4): *(from
  somewhere down the gulch, echoing)* **GEMMA:** "I'm FINE! This was always part
  of the plan!" / "Tell no one!"
- **Vesper idle on the trail:** *"Forty-one leaks filed. The form has a box for
  forty. …I'll need a second form."*
- **Thread Imp gag:** if an Imp follows Vesper after the fight — **VESPER:** "You
  don't work for her *or* me. …Fine. Keep up."

## Notes for when we wire it (later)

- Q2 (gem shards) and Q3 (Gemma) interleave but are independent: you can clear the
  horde/grab shards before triggering Gemma at the overlook.
- The Gemma fight needs **no real boss system** for a first pass: telegraphed
  projectiles + the slice stub + her shield-drops-on-monologue is greyboxable.
  Phase 2 is cuttable if the fight fights us (STORY/design page scope note).
- Keep **one** dread beat on this map (S3's "filing" line). Everything else cozy
  or funny. The real dread is saved for Whisper Gulch (Map 3).
- "The throne was fine, Gemma" and "land on something soft" are the **keepers** —
  the lines that make the rivalry warm instead of mean. Protect them in any trim.
