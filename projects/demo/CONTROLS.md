- **Move:** Left/Right arrows
- **Jump:** Space; press again in the air to flash-jump, or hold Up while
  pressing Space for an up-jump
- **Drop through a one-way platform:** Down + Space
- **Hop / flutter:** E; hold E in the air to flutter
- **Grapple:** Q
- **Teleport:** R
- **Slice:** D

Walk into a portal door to travel between rooms. Collect the coins; touching
spikes bounces you back to the room's start.
