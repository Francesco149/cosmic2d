- **Move:** Left/Right arrows, or the d-pad / left stick on a gamepad
- **Jump:** Space (pad: the bottom face button); press again in the air to
  flash-jump, or hold Up while pressing Space for an up-jump
- **Drop through a one-way platform:** Down + Space
- **Hop / flutter:** E (pad: the right face button); hold it in the air to
  flutter
- **Grapple:** Q (pad: the left shoulder)
- **Teleport:** R (pad: the top face button)
- **Slice:** D (pad: the left face button)

Every action is rebindable from the Esc menu's controls page. Walk into a
portal door to travel between rooms. Collect the coins; touching spikes
bounces you back to the room's start.
