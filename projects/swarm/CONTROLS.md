- **Move:** Arrow keys or WASD, or the d-pad / left stick on a gamepad
- **Fire:** Space (pad: the right shoulder) — shots fly along your facing,
  eight ways; on a gamepad the right stick aims, and aiming fires by itself
- **Restart:** R (pad: Start), or Fire after the swarm gets you

Every action is rebindable from the Esc menu's controls page. Kite the
waves, keep shooting, and chase the high score — it persists between runs.
