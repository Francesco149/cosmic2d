- **Move:** Arrow keys or WASD, or the d-pad / left stick on a gamepad —
  the stick is analog, so easing into a direction moves you slower
- **Interact:** E or Space (pad: the bottom face button) — unlocks the
  cellar door once the key is in hand
- **Restart:** R (pad: Start)

Every action is rebindable from the Esc menu's controls page. Find the key
in the cellar, unlock the door, cross the vault, stand on the pressure
plate to open the gate, and take the gem.
